Crypto and Card Payments

Payment choice affects both deposits and withdrawals. Crypto can be convenient for users who already understand wallets and network fees, while cards and bank routes feel more familiar but may involve issuer checks.

A safer approach is to confirm the deposit method, account name, bonus status and verification requirements before adding funds. Withdrawal delays often come from missing documents or unmatched payment ownership.

  • Double-check wallet networks and addresses.
  • Keep screenshots or records of payment confirmations.
  • Complete requested verification before large cashouts.
  • Do not use third-party payment methods.
